Rules of the "Construction Quiz 2025" Contest

The contest is organized by DMD Domy. It is held on DMD Domy’s Facebook and Instagram profiles. Meta (Facebook/Instagram) is not a sponsor or administrator of the contest and holds no responsibility for its course.

1. Contest Duration

The contest runs from April 1, 2025, to May 31, 2025, and is divided into two stages:
- Stage I: April 1–30, 2025 – prize: hammer drill
- Stage II: May 1–31, 2025 – prize: angle grinder

2. How to Participate

To participate in the contest:
- Like the DMD Domy profile on Facebook or Instagram,
- Answer the quiz questions by commenting with your choice (A/B/C/D),
- Each correct answer earns 1 ticket for the prize draw.

3. Winner Selection

The winner will be selected based on the number of tickets earned – the more correct answers, the higher the chance of winning. In case of a tie or dispute, the organizer reserves the right to conduct a random draw.

4. Results Announcement

Winners of each stage will be announced on DMD Domy’s social media profiles within 5 business days after the stage ends.

5. Prizes

- Stage I: Hammer drill
- Stage II: Angle grinder
Prizes cannot be exchanged for cash or substituted for other items.

6. Personal Data Protection

Participants' personal data will be processed solely for the purpose of running the contest and delivering the prize. Data will not be shared with third parties.

7. Final Provisions

The organizer reserves the right to change the rules or end the contest at any time, with notice given via social media. The full version of the rules is available at: www.dmdbudownictwo.pl/konkurs-quiz-2025
