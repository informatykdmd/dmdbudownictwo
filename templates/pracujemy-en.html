The investment process at DMD begins with thorough planning. At this stage, the investor (an individual client or a group of investors) initiates the idea for an investment, which can involve building a house, a commercial facility, or another type of structure. Our DMD Inwestycje team conducts a detailed analysis, evaluating the investment's potential, location, and project requirements.

We then prepare an action plan that addresses all aspects of the project, including the timeline, budget, and resources.

Once the plan is ready, we proceed to the execution phase. DMD Budownictwo begins construction work in line with the approved project. Depending on the nature of the investment, various DMD brands may be involved in the process:

  • DMD Instalacje: Handles comprehensive installation solutions, such as electrical systems, plumbing, and HVAC systems.
  • DMD Domy: Focuses on the construction of houses, ensuring the highest quality and aesthetic standards.
  • DMD EliteHome: Creates unique, luxury properties tailored to individual client needs.

All work is carried out with the utmost care, utilizing modern technologies and materials. Our team monitors progress to ensure compliance with the schedule and budget.

After completing construction and installation work, the project moves to the finalization phase. We conduct detailed technical inspections to ensure all work has been completed to the highest standards. The investment is then handed over to the investor.

  • DMD Development: If the investment is a joint venture, DMD Development steps in to promote the property on the market. We handle tenant or client acquisition to finalize the investment in the most lucrative way, according to the investors' preferences.

This stage concludes the investment process, ensuring full client satisfaction and maximizing the investment's value.

  • What are the first steps in the investment process with DMD?
    The process begins with the investment opportunity, where the client or investor initiates an idea for a property. Then, our DMD Inwestycje team analyzes and plans the project, preparing a detailed action plan.
  • Which DMD brands are involved in the investment process?
    Depending on the nature of the project, we involve appropriate teams: DMD Budownictwo, DMD Inwestycje, DMD Instalacje, DMD Domy, and DMD EliteHome. Each brand plays a unique role in ensuring the highest quality execution.
  • Does DMD also handle construction installations?
    Yes, DMD Instalacje specializes in comprehensive installation solutions, such as electrical systems, plumbing, and HVAC systems, ensuring efficiency and safety for every project.
  • What happens after construction is completed?
    After construction and installation work is completed, we conduct detailed technical inspections to ensure everything meets the highest standards. The project is then handed over to the investor. If the project is a joint investment, DMD Development promotes the property on the market, finding tenants or buyers.
  • How can I start working with DMD on my project?
    Get in touch with our team, who will guide you through every stage of planning and executing your investment. Together, we can transform your ideas and dreams into successful projects that deliver value and satisfaction for years to come.
